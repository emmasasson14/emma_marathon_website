Weeks 13-16

 

Hello, hello!! It has definitely been quite a bit since I have last updated my running blog. Rest assured I have kept up with my training for the most part, but took some time away from the good 'ole blogging.

 

Now that I am back, I have some updates on my progress!

 

Over the past few weeks, I got a tad under the weather, and didn't keep up with as much strength and mobilty as I would have liked. During the end of Week 14, I could definitely feel more pain in my joints, i.e knees, ankles, and also my shins on my long run day.

 

This past week though, I kicked it back into gear, and felt the positive benefits of strength training for my running. Not only do I feel like a strong runner, but little aches and pains seem to just disappear the moment I go back to my gym workouts.

 

Some helpful exercises I make sure to include are: squats, single leg RDLs, weighted calf raises, and hip flexor strengthening (leg raises, band work and fire hydrants)

 

To top it all off, this week ended with my longest run yet logging 5.03 miles, so progress is HAPPENING!!

Total running mileage: 7.01 miles
